Ulrikken

Information about Ulriken

Ulriken (but also known as Ålreken from the middel ages) is the tallest Mountian, among the seven mountain's that surround the city of Bergen. It has a height of 643 meters (2110 ft.) above the sea level. Ulriken does also have an aerial tram that transports tourists and hikers to the top of the moutain. At the top you could either visit the café that has an amazing viewpoint of Bergen, or you can plan out which trail you and your companions should hike.

Warlock

Warlock

Warlocks are a class of Guardian who are best described as "warrior-scholars", they utilize their Traveler-given powers for both attack and defense. Warlocks specialize in ranged melee attacks and cast elemental spells to eliminate their enemies.

Hunter

Hunter

The Hunter is a type of Guardian who uses stealth, hand cannons, sidearms and long-ranged weapons to kill their enemies. Hunters tend to be taciturn, mostly keeping to themselves and using lightweight throwing knives and smoke to disorient their enemies. They also tend to create more havock if given the chance.

Titan

Titan

The Titan is a Guardian class which specializes in armor. The first Titans built and defended The Wall that protects The City, their heritage is rooted in strength, sacrifice, and mercilessness.
